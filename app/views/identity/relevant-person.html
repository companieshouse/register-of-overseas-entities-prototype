Who can request a code

You can request an agent assurance code if you, or the company you work for, are a UK-regulated agent completing identity checks for overseas entities registering with Companies House.

A UK-regulated agent can be an individual or a corporate entity. For example:

  • credit institutions
  • financial institutions
  • auditors, insolvency practitioners, external accountants and tax advisers
  • independent legal professionals
  • trust or company service providers
  • estate agents and letting agents

They must be based in the UK and supervised under the Money Laundering, Terrorist Financing and Transfer of Funds Regulations 2017.
