You cannot use this service

You can only request an authorisation code for the registration of overseas entities if you, or the company you work for, are a UK-regulated professional.

UK-regulated professionals must be based in the UK and supervised under the Money Laundering, Terrorist Financing and Transfer of Funds Regulations 2017.
