You cannot use this service

You can only request an agent assurance code for the registration of overseas entities if you, or the company you work for, are a UK-regulated agent.

UK-regulated agents must be based in the UK and supervised under the Money Laundering, Terrorist Financing and Transfer of Funds Regulations 2017.
