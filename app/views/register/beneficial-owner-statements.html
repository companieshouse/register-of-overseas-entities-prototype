Have any registrable beneficial owners been identified?

What is a registrable beneficial owner?
A beneficial owner is any legal body that has significant influence or control over the overseas entity. It can be an individual person, other legal entity, government or public authority.
A beneficial owner is registrable if they meet one or more of the conditions known as the natures of control (opens in a new tab).
