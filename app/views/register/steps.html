You are a UK-regulated agent and you are registering for the overseas entity

You are doing the verification checks and registering the overseas entity using our online service.

  1. Read the guidance and gather all information
    If relevant, also download and complete the Trust Excel document. (52KB)
  2. Check if someone from your organisation has already requested an agent assurance code.
    If they have then go to step 6 in this list
  3. Download the 'Request an agent assurance code' form. (ODT, 9KB)
    You get one code per organisation.

  4. Complete the agent assurance code request form and email it to agentcode@companieshouse.gov.uk
  5. Companies House sends your organisation’s agent assurance code via email (do not share outside of your organisation)
  6. Complete the verification checks (see your guidance)
  7. Register the overseas entity within three months of doing the verification checks
  8. Companies House emails you an automatic response email with your application number
  9. Companies House sends you the overseas entity number via email and confirms that the registration is successful
