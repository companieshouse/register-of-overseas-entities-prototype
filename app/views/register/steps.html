Steps to register an overseas entity

To start, you’ll need to tell us the name of the overseas entity you’re registering and who we can contact about the application.

You’ll enter the information for the rest of the application in stages.

  1. Complete a statement to confirm that verification checks have been completed
  2. Give details about the overseas entity
  3. Add beneficial owner details
  4. Add managing officer details (if relevant)
  5. Submit trust information (if relevant)

You can check your answers and make changes if you need to before you submit your application.
