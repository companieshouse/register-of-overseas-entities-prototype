Step by step: How to register an overseas entity

There are different steps to registering and this depends on who you are and who is registering for you. The process is also different if you are registering online or via paper form.

You are an agent and you are registering for the overseas entity

You are doing the identity checks and registering the overseas entity using our online service.

  1. Read the guidance and gather all data required
  2. Check if your organisation has already requested an assurance code, if they have then go to step 6 in this list
  3. Download the assurance code request form (one code per organisation)
  4. Email Companies House the completed assurance code request form
  5. Companies House emails you your organisation’s assurance code (do not share outside of your organisation)
  6. Complete the identity checks (see our guidance)
  7. Register the overseas entity and confirm identity checks information
  8. On completion, you will receive an automatic response email with your application number
  9. Companies House emails you to confirm if the registration is successful

You are the agent and you are registering for an overseas entity on paper

  1. Read the guidance and gather all data required
  2. Check if someone in your organisation has already requested an assurance code, if yes then skip to 6 in this list
  3. Download the assurance code request form (one code per organisation)
  4. Email Companies House the completed assurance code request form
  5. Companies House emails you your organisation’s assurance code (do not share outside of your organisation)
  6. Complete the identity checks (see our guidance)
  7. Contact Companies House to request a paper form
  8. Once received, register the overseas entity and confirm identity checks using the form
  9. Send in your completed form via post/email to Companies House
  10. You will receive an email with your application number
  11. Companies House emails you to confirm if the registration is successful

You are an agent and the overseas entity is registering

You are doing the identity checks only.

  1. Read the guidance and gather all data required
  2. Check if your organisation has already requested an assurance code, if they have then go to step 6 in this list
  3. Download the assurance code request form (one code per organisation)
  4. Email Companies House the completed assurance code request form
  5. Companies House emails you your organisation’s assurance code (do not share outside of your organisation)
  6. The overseas entity registers (this can happen whilst you complete 1-5)
  7. The overseas entity contacts you with their OE application number
  8. Download the identity checks form
  9. Complete the identity checks and add their OE application number to the form
  10. Email Companies House the completed identity checks form
  11. Companies House emails the overseas entity to confirm if the registration is successful

You are the overseas entity and an agent is registering for you

You will be asking a third-party agent to do your identity checks and register for you.

  1. Read the guidance and gather all data required
  2. Contact a third-party agent to let them know you want them to do your identity checks and your registration
  3. Provide all information required for the registration to the third party (see our guidance)
  4. Companies House emails you to confirm if the registration is successful

You are an overseas entity and you, or someone on behalf of you is registering

You or someone on behalf of you (not a third-party agent) will be registering using our online service and asking an agent to complete the identity checks

  1. Read the guidance and gather all data required
  2. Contact a third-party agent to let them know you want them to do your identity checks only
  3. Register the overseas entity (this is done before the agent can complete your identity checks)
  4. On completion, you will receive an email with your application number
  5. Give this application number and all information required for identity checks to your third-party agent
  6. Companies House emails you to confirm if the registration is successful

You are the overseas entity and you are registering using our paper form

You or someone on behalf of you (not a third-part agent) will be registering using our paper form and asking an agent to complete the identity checks

  1. Read the guidance and gather all data required
  2. Contact a third-party agent to let them know you want them to do your identity checks only
  3. Email Companies House to request a paper form
  4. Once received, register the overseas entity using the form
  5. Send in your completed form via post/email to Companies House
  6. You will receive an email with your application number
  7. Give this application number and all information required for identity checks to your third-party agent
  8. Companies House emails you to confirm if the registration is successful
