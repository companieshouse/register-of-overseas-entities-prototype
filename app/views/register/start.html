Use this service to add an overseas entity and its beneficial owners or managing officers to the Register of Overseas Entities. This will allow it to buy and sell land or property in the UK.

{% from "govuk/components/warning-text/macro.njk" import govukWarningText %} {{ govukWarningText({ text: "The beneficial owners of overseas entities who do not register could be fined up to £X per day, be put in prison for up to X years, and have difficulty buying or selling UK property or land.", iconFallbackText: "Warning" }) }}

You'll need:

  • to sign in to or create a Companies House account
  • a credit or debit card to pay the £X fee
  • to give us the name and email address of someone we can contact about the application

In the service you’ll be required to give us information about the overseas entity and its beneficial owners or managing officers. Read the guidance about registering an overseas entity to find out what information you'll need to provide.

You’ll also need to confirm that identity checks have been carried out on all beneficial owners and managing officers. These checks must be done by a third-party agent, such as an accountant or solicitor.
